Construction of the framework

Now you're going to connect the air table center box with the surrounding framework.

Step 1: U-formation framework

  • Cut the aluminium strut profile into two 48cm pieces and four pieces with a minimum of 20cm with a metallsaw. The length of the four pieces is depending on your height preferences. A minimum of 25 cm is needed to have enough space for the camera below the table. The recommended length is 40cm.

The following is done twice:

  • Place two nuts inside the two aluminium strut profile pieces with variable length, fixate on each an angle with a M5 screw (6mm) at one end. Now connect both with the 48 cm long aluminium strut profile by using nuts and a M5 screw (6mm).

Step 2: Applying the U-formation framework to the central air table

  • Place two nuts inside each 48 cm rod of the U-formation frameworks on one side which adjacent to the side with nuts already placed in (90 degrees to both rods which can vary in length), both sides work.

  • Now line these nuts up with the holes inside 480mm x 64mm plexiglass plates of the central air table box.

  • The holes in the 480mm x 326mm plexiglass plate need to point in the opposite direction as the two rods which can vary in length.

  • Connect the air table box and the U-formation framework with two M5x12mm screws from the inside of the air box in each nut.

Repeat this step with the second U-formation framework on the other side of the air table box.

Step 3: Attach a second frame circle

To guarantee a higher stability it's recommended to place a second framework circle 5-10cm above the ground.

  • Therefore cut the aluminium strut profile in two pieces with a length of 44cm and three with a length of 32.6cm.

  • Place on both ends of each aluminium strut profile a nut.

  • Each 44cm aluminium strut profile gets an extra nut which needs to point to the center later.

  • Connect the nut and an angle with a M5 screw (6mm). Place nuts at the wished height inside the to the Airtable mounted aluminium strut profile.

  • Now connect the aluminium strut profiles with angles to the at the Airtable mounted aluminium strut profile with a M5 screw (6mm).

  • Put two nuts inside the third 32.6cm aluminium strut profile. These both need to point upwards in the next step.

  • Place the third 32.6cm aluminium strut profile in the center at the two unused nuts and connect it with two more M5 screw (6mm).

picture

Step 4: Glueing of the bottom plate

After connecting the air table with the framework there is no need in accessing the inner box.

  • Glue the second 480mm x 326mm plexiglass plate on the bottom side of the already build. Line up the long side of the box with the long side of the plate, there must be no hole for air flowing through.

  • Again it's necessary to degrease and remove any dirt at the glueing surface before glueing.

You have build the center air table now.
