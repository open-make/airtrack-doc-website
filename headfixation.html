Construction of the head fixation

The animal will be head fixed in the center of the platform. Here is a description of the setup construction using XXX head fixation method.

If the head fixation system you have is incompatible with this construction, a placement on the vertical M5 thread rod might work.

Parts

  • 1 aluminium rod 12mm diameter
  • 2 aluminium strut profile
  • 1 center air table
  • 2 connecting clamp
  • 2 M5 round rods
  • 6 nuts

Tools

  • 1 metallsaw

Step 1: Placing vertical mounting

  • Place three nuts in each 480 mm aluminium strut profile of the center air table, pointing upwards.

  • Move the central nuts to the center of the two 480 mm aluminium strut profiles.

  • Place in each central nut one of the M5 round rods. Attach on each M5 round rod a connecting clamp 150 mm above the table.

Step 2: Attaching the head fixation

  • Place one connecting clamp on each of the upwards pointing rods in a 90 degree angle.
