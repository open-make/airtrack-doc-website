Construction of the rewarding system

Additional framework

The following modification will be placed on the opposite of the pressure relief valve.

Place two outside pointing nuts in each aluminium strut profile. Place one below the lower framework and one close to the top of the aluminium strut profile. Attach to all an angle by using M5 screws (6mm). The angles must point to each other. Attach to both sides vertical an aluminium strut profile which raises 25cm above the airtable by using M5 screws (6mm) and nuts. The exact length is depending on your choices of the general height.

Now place two nuts in each vertical aluminium strut profile. One nut in each aluminium strut profile needs point to each other. Attach to these nuts an angle by using M5 screws (6mm). The other nuts need to point towards the center of the air table. Cut one aluminium strut profiles with a length of 32,6cm and place it horizontal at the vertical aluminium strut profiles pointing to the air table center. It should be 5cm above the air table an the bottom side. Place in the horizontal aluminium strut profile two more nuts which need to point upwards later. Use more M5 screws (6mm) and nuts to connect it with the upper angles and place it towards the inside.

Platform borders

Use the lower, just placed nuts to add a 286mm x 30mm plexiglass plate. Align both 3cm high endings with the edges of the vertical aluminium strut profiles and mark the center of the aluminium strut profiles on the plexiglass . Predill with 1,5cm distance to the edges two holes with a 5mm drill at the marks. Align the nuts with holes. Fixate the 286mm x 30mm plexiglass plate with two M5 screws (12mm).

Use the remaining nuts in the upper 48cm long aluminium strut profiles next to the head fixation rods and place two 286mm x 30mm plexiglass plates in the center of side by using four M5 screws (12mm), four M5 screws (6mm) and four angles.

To achieve a border at the side of the pressure relieve valve place a nut at this side in the top framework edge and attach an angle to each nut by using a M5 screw (6mm). Cut a 32,6cm long aluminium strut profile and use two more nuts and M5 screws (6mm) to fixate it.

Linear actuator

Place Linear Actuator on the lower horizontal aluminium strut profile and fixate it to the upwards pointing nuts by using the included mount with M5 screws (6mm).

Solenoid pinch valve

Place one solenoid pinch valve at the top of each vertical aluminium strut profile. Therefore place a nut at the top of each aluminium strut profile and fixate a pinch valve clamp on each with a M5 screw (6mm). Place the solenoid pinch valve inside the pinch valve clamp.
