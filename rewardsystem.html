Construction of the rewarding system

Step 1: Additional framework

The following modification will be placed on the opposite of the pressure relief valve.

  • Place two outside pointing nuts in each vertical aluminium strut profile. Place one below the lower framework and one close to the top of the aluminium strut profile.

  • Attach to all an angle by using M5 screws (6mm). The angles must point to each other.

  • Attach to both sides vertical an aluminium strut profile which raises 25cm above the airtable by using M5 screws (6mm) and nuts.

    • The exact length is depending on your choices of the general height.

  • Now place one nut in each just attached, vertical aluminium strut profile. The nut in each aluminium strut profile needs to point to each other.

    • Attach to these nuts an angle by using M5 screws (6mm). The unattached side needs to point towards the air table.

    • Cut one aluminium strut profiles with a length of 32,6cm and place it horizontal at the vertical aluminium strut profiles pointing to the air table.

    • Use two nuts and two M5 screws (6mm) to fixate the aluminium strut profiles.

    • The lower edge should be at the height of the air table surface.

Step 2: Platform borders

  • Place three nuts in each upper aluminium strut profiles of the U-formation frame.

    • Insert them from the pressure reliefe side.

  • Use the deepest two nuts and place them with a distance of 15 cm from the center in both directions. Attach an angle to each by using M5 screws (6mm).

    • The angles should point towards the air table.

  • Fixate the 350mm x 30mm plexiglass plate with two M5 screws (12mm) and two M5 hexagon nuts at the angles on both sides.

    • Therefore mark and predrill the spots of the angle holes at the plexiglass for the screws with a 5mm drill.

  • Place the remaining nuts at the end of the aluminium strut profiles of the U-formation frame.

  • Attach two angles to the nuts by using M5 screws (6mm).

    • The angles should point to the outside.

  • Mark the angle holes at the 366mm x 30mm plexiglass plate and predrill them with a 5mm drill.

  • Attach the 366mm x 30mm plexiglass plate by using two M5 screws (12mm) and two M5 hexagon nuts.

Step 3: Linear actuator

  • Choose one long side of the U-formation framework and drill a 1/4" thread inside the upper aluminium strut profiles on the opposite side of the pressure relief valve.

    • Keep a 5 cm distance from the end of the aluminium strut profiles.

  • Place the SmallRig articulated_arm in the thread with one side.

  • On the other side of the SmallRig articulated arm place the SmallRig super clamp.

  • Place the linear actuator in the SmallRig Super Clamp close to the center end of the not moving part.

    • The front hole in the linear actuator needs to point upwards.

  • Move the articulated arm and linear actuator to have the linear actuator orientated along the long side and centered. The moveable end needs to point towards the pressure relief valve. The super clamp should be around 10 cm above the table and 5 cm from the short airtable edge. The linear actuator should point around 30Â° downwards.

Step 4: Solenoid pinch valve

Place one solenoid pinch valve at the top of each vertical aluminium strut profile.

  • Therefore place a nut at the top of each aluminium strut profile and fixate a pinch valve clamp on each with a M5 screw (6mm).

  • Place the solenoid pinch valve inside the pinch valve clamp.
