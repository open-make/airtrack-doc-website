Airtable_1: Predrill and precut

Parts

  • 1 480mm x 326mm plexiglass plate
  • 2 480mm x 64mm plexiglass plates

Tools

  • 1 5mm plexiglas drill
  • 1 8mm plexiglas drill

Step 1: Predrill of Top plexiglass plates

  • Drill 28 holes (0.8 cm diameter) into the 480mm x 326mm plexiglass plate, as shown in the following drawing, with a 8mm plexiglas drill.

    • 4 columns: 5.4, 10.4, 22.2, 27.2 cm of the border
    • 7 raws: 6.3, 12.2, 18.1, 24.0, 29.9, 36.8, 41.7 cm of the border

  • Remove remains of drilling.

  • Now you have a predrilled 480mmx326mm plexiglass plate.
