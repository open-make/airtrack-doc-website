Airtable part 2: Predrill and precut

Parts

  • 2 480mm x 326mm plexiglass plate
  • 2 480mm x 64mm plexiglass plates

Tools

  • 1 3mm plexiglas drill
  • 1 5mm plexiglas drill
  • 1 8mm plexiglas drill

Step 1: Predrill of Top plexiglass plates

  • Drill 28 holes (8 mm diameter) into the 480mm x 326mm plexiglass plate, as shown in the following drawing, with a 8mm plexiglas drill.

  • Additional drill 28 holes (3 mm diameter) into the 480mm x 326mm plexiglass plate, as shown in the following drawing, with a 3mm plexiglas drill.

Constructional drawing (distances in millimeter)

  • Remove remains of drilling.

  • Now you have a predrilled 480mmx326mm plexiglass plate.

Step 2: Predrill long sides

To connect the air table with the table legs, the long sides (480mm x 64mm plexiglass plates) of the central box needs connection points.

  • Drill a hole on each end of both 480mm x 64mm plexiglass plates with a 5mm plexiglas drill.

  • Keep a distance of 45 mm to the short edge and 10 mm to the top. You can choose which side it at the top for you but you need to keep this orientation.

  • Drill two more holes inside both 480mm x 64mm plexiglass plates at the same height with a distance of 130 mm from the short edge towards the plate's center.

  • Now you have two identical predrilled 480x64 plexiglass plate.
