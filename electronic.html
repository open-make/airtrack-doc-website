Set up of the electric system

Parts

  • 1 Active Buzzer
  • 1 Arduino Uno
  • many cable ties
  • 1 capacitive sensor mpr121
  • 1 circuit board
  • 1 H bridge L293D

Step 1: Wiring of devices

  • In order to connect the devices you need a circuit board, e.g. this circuit board.

  • Further you need many jumper wires to set up connections accordingly to the following circuit drawing. You need male, female and ordinary wires.

  • To clean up the pathing of cables you can use cable ties like, e.g. these cable ties and fixate with that the cables at the aluminium strut profile.

  • To keep a structure between the Arduino Uno, capacitive sensor mpr121, Active Buzzer and H bridge L293D you can use a large breadboard.

Wiring configuration

Step 2: Mpr121 - capacitive sensor

The left and right sensor in the circuit diagram is meant to be a electrically conductive wire which is attached to each needle.

  • The wire needs to be wrapped around the needle multiple times
    • For small changes in the placement of the wire the threshold of the Mpr121 needs to be adjusted. The margin of tolerance is small.
